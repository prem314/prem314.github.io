Hyperbolic Maze Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 20, 2026

Hyperbolic Maze is an offline maze puzzle game.

Data collection

The app does not collect personal information. It does not use accounts, analytics, advertising SDKs, location services, contacts, camera, microphone, or server sync.

Data stored on the device

The app stores campaign progress locally so unlocked levels and best star results can be restored. This progress is not sent to the developer or to a server.

Sharing

When the user taps Share after escaping a level, the app creates a PNG image locally and opens Android's system share sheet. The user chooses whether to share it and which app receives it.

Data removal

To remove local progress, clear the app data in Android settings or uninstall the app.

Contact

For privacy questions, email premkr.game.dev@gmail.com.
