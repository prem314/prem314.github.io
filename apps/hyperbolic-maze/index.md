# Hyperbolic Maze

Hyperbolic Maze is a lightweight portrait puzzle game built on the regular hyperbolic {4,5} tiling. Swipe through curved passages, collect gems, use limited hints, and escape across 50 campaign levels.

- Offline gameplay
- No ads
- No in-app purchases
- No account required
- No analytics or personal-data collection
