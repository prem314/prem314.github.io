Privacy Policy For Chaos Pendulum Archer

Effective date: 2026-06-21

Chaos Pendulum Archer is a lightweight physics arcade game. Its core gameplay is designed to work offline.

Information Provided By Players

The app does not require an account and does not ask for a name, email address, or other profile information. The developer does not operate a server that receives gameplay progress or gameplay analytics.

Data Stored On Device

The app may store local progress such as level stars and high scores. This information stays on the device and is used only to show progress inside the game. It can be removed by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.

Advertising

The app displays a banner supplied by Google AdMob. The Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect and share information such as IP address, approximate location derived from IP address, app interactions, diagnostic information, advertising ID, app set ID, and other device identifiers. Google uses this information for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention purposes.

Google's advertising and privacy practices are described at:

  • https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Consent And Privacy Choices

The app uses Google's User Messaging Platform. Where required, players can consent to advertising data use, refuse consent, or manage individual choices before an ad is requested. Eligible players can reopen Privacy choices from the Levels screen. Refusing consent may result in limited ads where supported.

Data Security

Google states that data collected by the Google Mobile Ads SDK is encrypted in transit using TLS. The developer does not maintain a separate remote database of player information.

Purchases

The app does not contain in-app purchases.

Network Access

Core gameplay works offline. Advertising and consent services require network access and may be unavailable when the device is offline.

Children

The game is intended as a general-audience arcade game and is not directed specifically at young children. The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children through an account or gameplay server.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the developer contact email listed on the app's Google Play Store page.
