Privacy Policy For Chaos Pendulum Archer

Effective date: 2026-05-26

Chaos Pendulum Archer is a lightweight offline physics arcade game.

Data Collection

The app does not collect personal information.

The app does not require an account, does not ask for a name or email address, and does not send gameplay data to a server.

Data Stored On Device

The app may store simple local game progress on the user's device, such as level stars or best scores. This information stays on the device and is used only to show the player's progress inside the game.

Data Sharing

The app does not share user data with third parties.

Ads And Purchases

The app does not contain ads.

The app does not contain in-app purchases.

Network Access

The app is designed to run offline. It does not require network access for normal gameplay.

Children

The app is a simple arcade game and does not knowingly collect data from children or any other users.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the developer contact email listed on the app's Google Play Store page.
